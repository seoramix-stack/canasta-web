Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Canasta Master Club ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and web service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Username: Your chosen display name
  • Password: Stored securely using bcrypt encryption
  • Email: If provided during registration

2.2 Game Data

  • Game statistics (wins, losses, ELO rating)
  • Match history and gameplay data
  • Friends list and social interactions

2.3 Payment Information

Important: We do NOT store your credit card information. All payments are processed securely through Stripe's web checkout. When you subscribe to Premium, you are redirected to Stripe's secure payment page. We only receive confirmation of successful payments.

2.4 Technical Data

  • IP address (for security and rate limiting)
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Authenticate your account and prevent fraud
  • Display leaderboards and match players
  • Process Premium subscriptions (via Stripe)
  • Improve game balance and user experience
  • Send important service notifications

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Stripe (Payment Processing)

Premium subscriptions are processed through Stripe. When you click "Subscribe", you are redirected to Stripe's secure checkout page. Please review Stripe's Privacy Policy.

4.2 MongoDB Atlas (Database)

Your account data and game statistics are stored on MongoDB Atlas servers. MongoDB complies with GDPR and industry-standard security practices.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted password storage (bcrypt)
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • JWT token-based authentication
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Secure database connections

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Portability: Export your game statistics

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@canastamaster.club

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Users

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using our service, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via in-app notification or email.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

Email: privacy@canastamaster.club
